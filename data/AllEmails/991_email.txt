Alert Posted: 11:30 AM November 21, 2000: Demand Mismatch Order update
Effective Wednesday, November 22, 2000 at 10:00 a.m., AGLC is lifting the 
Demand Mismatch Orders for all primary pools in AGLC areas.
However, the Chattanooga Gas Customers are still under curtailment orders 
until further notice.
